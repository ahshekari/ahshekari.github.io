About the Client

Cooks For Hire, founded in 2007 and based in Vancouver, BC, specializes in staffing chefs and kitchen staff for restaurants, events, and temporary hospitality needs. With 250+ casual employees and expansion across Canada, CFH wanted to modernize internal systems with automation and better integration between tools like Makeshift, ADP, QuickBooks, Zoho, and Zapier.

Project Objectives & Challenges

  • Disconnected tools requiring manual data entry between platforms.
  • Zapier automations frequently broke or were underutilized.
  • No centralized workflow visibility or tracking.
  • Inefficient communication between systems and staff.
  • Time-consuming admin tasks limited focus on growth.

Our Role

As part of Team Planist, I helped analyze workflows, identify gaps, and recommend solutions to streamline CFH‚Äôs operations:

  • Conducted process mapping, SWOT analysis, and system evaluation.
  • Recommended automation tools (Make.com and Zapier) for high-impact tasks.
  • Suggested phased implementation for minimizing disruption.

Solutions Implemented

  • Automation: Connected tools via Zapier and Make.com for onboarding, scheduling, and payroll syncing.
  • CRM Integration: Used Zoho Forms + CRM to centralize client and employee data, reduce duplicate entry, and automate follow-ups.
  • WhatsApp Integration: Linked WhatsApp Business with Zoho CRM for faster client communications.
  • Data Compliance: Evaluated hosting (Make.com EU vs Zapier US) to ensure privacy and GDPR alignment.

Impact

  • Reduced manual data entry by automating shift confirmations and onboarding.
  • Centralized client & employee data for improved tracking.
  • Improved scalability for future automations (e.g., job matching, lifecycle tracking).
  • Created roadmap for staff training, troubleshooting, and compliance monitoring.

Next Steps

  • Transition from Zapier to Make.com for advanced automation.
  • Train CFH staff to monitor and maintain automations.
  • Expand Zoho CRM usage for sales pipeline and client lifecycle.
